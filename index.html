About

Why the Name Brihaspati ?

The name Brihaspati is inspired by Hindu mythology, where Brihaspati is the guru (teacher) of the gods, symbolizing wisdom, intelligence, and deep knowledge. These characteristics align closely with the purpose of the Brihaspati forensic tool.

Wisdom & Intelligence – Brihaspati is known for his vast knowledge and guidance. Similarly, the tool leverages AI to intelligently analyze media files, detect prohibited content, and extract crucial forensic data.

Truth & Justice – As the guru of the gods, Brihaspati represents truth and righteousness. The forensic tool serves a similar purpose by uncovering hidden evidence, ensuring justice, and assisting in digital investigations.

Data Discovery & Analysis – Just as Brihaspati provides insight and clarity, the tool retrieves media files, chat messages, and transaction details, revealing critical information for cybersecurity and forensic experts.

By naming the tool Brihaspati, it embodies the essence of wisdom-driven digital forensics, enabling investigators to uncover the truth with precision and intelligence.

Data Extraction

Brihaspati is an advanced cybersecurity forensic tool designed to facilitate comprehensive data retrieval from connected mobile devices. It enables forensic professionals to extract and analyze critical digital evidence with precision and efficiency.

Extracted Data Categories:

Media Files – Retrieval of photos, videos, and other multimedia content.

Chat Messages – Extraction of communication logs from messaging applications.

Bank Transactions – Collection of financial transaction records for forensic examination.

AI Detection - Retrieving photos of prohibited items in india using artificial intelligence.

Data Recovery

Brihaspati incorporates advanced data recovery techniques to retrieve lost, deleted, or inaccessible digital evidence from mobile devices. This capability is essential for forensic investigations, ensuring that critical information remains recoverable even after intentional or accidental deletion.

Key Features of Data Recovery:

Deleted Media Retrieval – Recovers lost or deleted photos, videos, and other multimedia files.

Report Generation

Brihaspati automatically creates detailed reports by compiling extracted data, AI analysis, and detected issues. These reports are secure, well-structured, and easy to understand. They can be saved in PDF formats making documentation simple and reliable.

Key Feature:

Clear and secure reports with AI-powered insights for investigations.
